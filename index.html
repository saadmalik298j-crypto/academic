The Shortlisting Filter

Why academic consistency is your first ticket into the interview room.

MNC Requirements

Major tech giants often set a baseline of 6.5 to 7.5 CGPA to manage the sheer volume of applicants. It acts as a proxy for discipline and reliability.

Startup Agility

High-growth startups might be more flexible, but a strong CGPA (8.0+) still serves as a signal of high performance and fast learning capabilities.

CGPA vs Skills – The Recruiter's Lens

Recruiters today look for "T-Shaped" individuals. While your CGPA opens the door, your technical and soft skills close the deal. The ideal candidate balance looks like this:

Academic Excellence

Signals: Consistency, Discipline, Foundational Knowledge.

Hands-on Skills

Signals: Problem-solving, Adaptability, Domain Expertise.

Placement Quick Wins

Small adjustments that yield massive results during the hiring cycle.

  • Build a project that solves a real-world problem, not just a tutorial clone.
  • Master Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) – it's the core of tech interviews.
  • Optimize your LinkedIn profile with relevant keywords and a professional headshot.
  • Practice "STAR" method for behavioral interviews (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
  • Keep a clean repo on GitHub to showcase your coding journey.
